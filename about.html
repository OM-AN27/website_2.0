about

Born and raised in Guadalajara, Mexico. I moved to Canada seeking personal and career growth.


Growing up with a tech savvy dad, who always knew computers would be part of his kids future, at young age I learned the very basics of a computer. Spending hours staring to a screen trying to understand why it did the things it did. Together with my already growing passion for design, I always wanted to get a job on something that will combine both.


Fueled by gummy bears and a mind full of creativity, I signed up for two different Web Dev programs in Canada and started my journey in designing and creating web pages.


What I know today might not be enough for tomorrow. What I will learn tomorrow will be enough to face those obstacles.
